About

The "Elite Dangerous Squadrone Tracker" (EDST) is a programm provided by the EDDS (Elite Dangerous Data Service) with the intention to track various statistics about your squadron members.

Data-Provider

EDST allows you to use Spansh, EDSM or EDDS as data provider for information about Elite.
Be aware that informations about your squadrone members are always based of EDDS`s data.
This is because most of the member data is calculated by EDDS`s API and will as such use the EDDS database.
